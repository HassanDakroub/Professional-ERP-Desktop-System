# Professional ERP Desktop System

> A modern desktop ERP solution developed for inventory management, manufacturing, finance, customer management, and business operations.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Professional ERP Desktop System is an offline desktop application designed to help businesses manage daily operations efficiently.

The application combines inventory management, manufacturing workflows, supplier management, finance, customer management, reporting, and intelligent material optimization in a single desktop platform.

---

# ✨ Main Features

- 📊 Dashboard & Business Analytics
- 👥 Customer Management
- 📦 Inventory & Stock Management
- 🏭 Aluminium Manufacturing
- 📄 Invoice Management
- 🚚 Supplier Management
- 💰 Finance & Expenses
- 🏦 Bank Management
- 📑 Reports
- 📥 PDF Export
- 📗 Excel Export
- 🔍 Search & Filtering

---

# 🧠 Smart Manufacturing

One of the main features of the system is the intelligent material optimization engine.

It automatically generates:

- Glass Cutting Plans
- Aluminium Profile Cutting Plans
- Material Requirements
- Waste Reduction Calculations

to improve production efficiency and reduce material waste.
